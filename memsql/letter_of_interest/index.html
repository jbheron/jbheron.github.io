Hello, Folks at MemSQL!

I'm Jess. I like to write and I think we should work together.



What about me?

As of August 2015 I have been documenting all things Cloud at Pivotal.


I am a tech writer.

For the last several years I have been happily writing technical documents, developing web content, and I got my technical chops at Dev Bootcamp. I have made web apps, manuals, movies, houses, furniture, commercials, and one screenplay. I've always been a craftsman of one sort or another.


And, before that?

I was trained in the creative and technical craft of film making, but early on found myself building houses on the far away island of Nantucket.


Why? Because life is one big, amazing adventure.

After enough blustery winters to appreciate good weather, I traded my beard and hammer for a bag of hopes and a pen in Hollywood. When I met my future wife, we waved goodbye to LA and headed homeward-- back to the Bay Area for us both!


What do I value?

I bring years of experience in the technical contribution to the communication, implementation and vision of products in technology. I value empathy, humor, clarity, transparency and teamwork, and I hope you do too!
