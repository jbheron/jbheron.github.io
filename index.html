A JOURNEY OF BUILDING WITH PASSION

Writer • Thinker • Maker

As of August 2015 I have been documenting all things Cloud at Pivotal.

I was trained in the creative and technical craft of film making, but early on found myself building houses on the far away island of Nantucket. Why? Because life is a big, fat amazing ride.

After enough blustery winters to appreciate good weather, I traded my beard and hammer for a bag of hopes and a pen in Hollywood. When I met my future wife, we waved goodbye to LA and headed homeward-- back to the Bay Area!

For the last several years I have been happily developing web content, writing technical documents and I got some technical chops at Dev Bootcamp. I've always been a craftsman of one sort or another. I have made web apps, manuals, movies, houses, furniture, commercials, and one screenplay.

I bring years of experience in the technical contribution to the communication, implementation and vision of products in technology. I value empathy, humor, clarity, transparency and teamwork. I can be reached through any of the contact links at this site.
